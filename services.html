Sea Freight Services

Whether you have full container load (FCL), less than container load
(LCL) or non-containerised load, we have the sea freight solutions you
need for your cargo shipping.

Air Freight Services

BAJA TRANS offers reliable services that help transport your cargo
with short amount of time. We will keep your goods moving with

Warehousing

We provide warehousing services to store your goods or act as
distribution center to distributre your products with our
well-equiped and secured warehouse management locations. With
strategic positioned warehouses, we made sure your supply-chain
is safe and smooth. With up to date security solutions and
techonology that we invent, everything is just a tap away at
your mobile phone.

Export Import Customs Clearance

Whatever your needs, BAJA TRANS provide 360-degree import,
export and customs clearance services at ease. BAJA TRANS will
work hard to minimize paperwork, expediting transit, reduce transit
costs to existing/new importers and exporters. We aim to make
export-import simple and straightforward so that anyone can
export-import anything from anywhere.

Stevedoring Services

Our BAJATRANS stevedoring team load and unload cargos by
operating heavy machinery such as heavy load cranes, gantry cranes,
and other vehicles like reach stackers to lift containers, load cargo and transport it.
